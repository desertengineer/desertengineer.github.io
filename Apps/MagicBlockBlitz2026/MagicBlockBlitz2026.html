About The Game

Step into a dystopian cyber grid where speed is your ultimate shield. Cyber Neon Strike combines old-school responsive arcade reflex mechanics with gorgeous modern visual effects and a pulsing synthwave soundtrack.

As a rogue cyber-runner, you must slice, dash, and parse through firewalls and defense modules to extract vital legacy data blocks. Every level speeds up, demanding absolute muscle memory execution.

Key Features

  • 🔥 High-Octane Combat: Fluid 60FPS input-buffered dash and slash mechanics.
  • 🎵 Procedural Synth Soundtrack: Audio dynamically adapts to your combo multipliers.
  • 🛠️ Deep Customization: Over 45 upgrade nodes to fully fine-tune your loadout matrix.
  • 🏆 Global Leaderboards: Race against real-time ghosts of global top players.
